GYNECOLOGY

KG Hospital's Obstetrics & Gynecology Department is a tertiary care facility which provides enhanced OB/GY care.
This includes a state-of-the-art comprehensive Neonatal Intensive Care Unit for the newborn babies.
Apart from managing the routine problems, Gynaecological Cancer Screening, Infertility Workup Protocols, treatment and follow-up of menopausal or hysterectomized women with Hormone Replacement Therapy are some of the services provided by the Department.
The Department has a fullfledged Obstetric setup, including a fully equipped labour theatre, with Fetal Cardio Tachometer, an operation theatre functioning round-the-clock, ICUs and CCUs for obstetric criticare.
